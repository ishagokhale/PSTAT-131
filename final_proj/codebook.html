1. charges:  The outcome variable describing the amount an individual has to pay in medical insurance in dollars yearly. Type: double

2. age: The age of the primary beneficiary.  Type: integer

3. sex: The gender of the insurance contractor, either male (‘M’) or female (‘F’). 

4. bmi: Body mass index of primary beneficiary. Type: double

5. children: Number of children covered by the health insurance (also known as number of dependents). Takes on integer values of 0-5.

6. smoker: Whether the person smokes or not: ‘yes’ or ‘no’

7. region: Beneficiary's location of residence in the US: ‘northeast’, ‘southeast’, ‘southwest’, or ‘northwest’
